About Us

We are a pet shop dedicated to catering for all your pets needs. Any animal big or small is welcome in our store each day. We also offer discounts on purchases over €10. For every €10 you spend you get a stamp on your loyalty card and when you have 10 stamps collected, you get a free €10 off voucher to use in store. Please also feel free to avail of our dog obedience classes and ask us any questions you have relating to your pet and we will be more than happy to help you.
